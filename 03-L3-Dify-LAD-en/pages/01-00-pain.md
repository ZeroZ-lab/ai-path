## 1.0 Pain Point: Why Is "Just Using ChatGPT" Not Enough?

<div class="grid gap-8 md:grid-cols-2 mt-6">
  <div v-click class="rounded-3xl border border-slate-200 bg-white shadow-lg p-6 transition-all duration-500">
    <div class="text-xs font-bold uppercase tracking-widest text-slate-400 mb-2">Direct Conversational Use</div>
    <h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-slate-900 mb-4">Everyone Can Ask, But Hard to Reuse</h3>
    <ul class="text-sm text-slate-600 space-y-3 list-disc pl-5">
      <li>Output varies by person: prompts and context depend entirely on individual experience.</li>
      <li>Process is uncontrollable: can't see how the model takes steps, hard to audit.</li>
      <li>Collaboration is difficult: same questions asked repeatedly, can't solidify into processes.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div v-click class="rounded-3xl border border-blue-200 bg-blue-50/60 shadow-inner p-6 transition-all duration-500">
    <div class="text-xs font-bold uppercase tracking-widest text-blue-500 mb-2">Platform-Based Delivery</div>
    <h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-blue-900 mb-4">Turn Experience into Reusable "Workflows"</h3>
    <ul class="text-sm text-blue-900/80 space-y-3 list-disc pl-5">
      <li>Controllable: node-based decomposition, prompts/variables/tools explicitly configured.</li>
      <li>Reusable: same workflow runs multiple times, consistent results across handoffs.</li>
      <li>Governable: permissions, logs, version management—AI can be audited.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>

<div v-click class="mt-8 rounded-2xl border border-emerald-100 bg-emerald-50/80 p-4 text-sm text-emerald-800 leading-relaxed transition-all duration-500">
  <strong>Conclusion:</strong> ChatGPT is great for inspiration and exploration; we need a platform to solidify "individual wisdom" into "enterprise assets."
</div>
